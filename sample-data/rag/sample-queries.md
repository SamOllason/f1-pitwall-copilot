# Sample RAG Queries (Demo Script)

Use these during a demo to show retrieval quality and grounded answers.

## Race Weekend Briefing Q&A

1. What were our biggest expected weaknesses at Silverstone in similar weather?
2. Which Silverstone sector looked most vulnerable and what caused it?
3. What setup direction was recommended for high-speed rear stability?
4. At Suzuka, what should we protect in the opening stint and why?
5. Which tire looked like the best race compromise at Suzuka and Silverstone?

## Driver Debrief Assistant

6. What recurring issues has Lando reported at Silverstone?
7. How does one rear instability event affect his next laps?
8. Which telemetry patterns support the confidence-drop hypothesis?
9. What engineering actions are suggested for the next similar circuit?
10. Summarize Lando's key feedback in three bullet points with sources.

## Filtered Query Examples

11. Only use `driver = Lando Norris`: what are the top two recurring handling issues?
12. Only use `race = Japanese Grand Prix`: what are the setup recommendations?
13. Only use `docType = race_briefing`: compare Silverstone vs Suzuka vulnerabilities.
